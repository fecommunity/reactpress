# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** July 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the **ReactPress official documentation site** (`docs.gaoredu.com`, operated as part of the ReactPress open-source project) handles information when you visit or use this website.

> This policy applies to the **documentation website only**. Sites you deploy with ReactPress are under your own control and policies.

## Information we collect

### Automatically collected data

When you browse this documentation site, standard web server and analytics services may collect:

- IP address (often anonymized)
- Browser type and version
- Pages visited and referral URL
- Approximate geographic region
- Device type and screen size

We use this data to understand traffic patterns, fix broken links, and improve documentation quality.

### Analytics

This site may use privacy-focused analytics (such as Vercel Analytics) that do not use third-party advertising cookies. Analytics data is aggregated and not sold to advertisers.

### Cookies

Docusaurus stores a **theme preference** (light/dark mode) in `localStorage`. No login is required to read public documentation.

## Information we do not collect

- We do not require an account to read docs
- We do not sell personal data
- We do not run third-party ad networks on this documentation site

## Third-party services

This site links to external services that have their own policies:

- [GitHub](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement) — source code and issue tracking
- [npm](https://www.npmjs.com/policies/privacy) — package downloads
- [Vercel](https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy) — optional hosting and analytics

## Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold. Contact us at [admin@gaoredu.com](mailto:admin@gaoredu.com) with your request.

## Changes

We may update this policy as the site evolves. Material changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
